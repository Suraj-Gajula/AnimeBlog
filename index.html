About AnimeBlog

AnimeBlog is a website for posting and reading literary analyses about anime and manga. This site is a work in progress. If you have any suggestions or come across any bugs please send me an email.

How to use AnimeBlog

By clicking the Account tab, you can either make a new account by entering an unregistered username and making a password or log in to an existing one by entering a registered username and it's associated password.

By clicking the Create tab once the Account tab shows you have logged in, you can make a post by typing out a title and a body. Once the post is finished, you'll automatically navigate tab where you can view your post by clicking the item with its information as well as posts others have made. You can also delete posts you have made back in the Account tab by pressing the trash button.

While viewing a post, you can either download it as a multi-page PDF by clicking the download button or read it online. You can also generate a MLA citation by clicking the cite paper button.

What to Post

Only post formal literary analyses on anime, manga, lns or vns. Don't post plagiarized content. Anything posted in violation of these rules is subject to deletion at my discretion.
